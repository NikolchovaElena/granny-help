Granny is a platform that help connect the generations and make a positive difference in the community. Our goal is to help as many elderly people possible by presenting volunteers with many great opportunities to choose from.

As a volunteer you can help by contributing your time or by making a donation. You can also contribute by staying vigilant and by registering new causes on our platform.

Many elderly people have technological difficulties and need assistance registering a cause. For that reason our volunteer opportunities are mainly faded to us by volunteers.

You don't need to have an account to register a cause with us. But we would love to have you in our family.
